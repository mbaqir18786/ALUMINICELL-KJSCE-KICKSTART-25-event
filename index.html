KICKSTART'25: A Mock Placement Drive

Organized by Alumni Cell KJSCE

About KICKSTART'25

Kickstart is a 3-day mock placement training event to equip you with the skills and experience needed to crack your dream job interview! It includes the three main ingredients of a Placement Drive - Aptitude Test, Group Discussions, and Personal Interviews, exclusively conducted by Alumni across different domains - Core, Technical, and Non-Technical.

Keeping in mind its importance, we give the students an opportunity to experience and develop their interview skill set well beforehand in time. What better than being personally groomed and mentored by our Alumni who have carved a benchmark for themselves in the corporate world!

Exclusive Opportunity: Outstanding performers stand a chance to secure internships with startups/companies founded by KJSCE Alumni!

Join us from 4th to 9th February 2025 for an immersive and interactive experience designed to simulate real placement processes. Here's what we've planned for you:

About Organizer

The Alumni Cell at KJSCE is a student-driven initiative dedicated to fostering strong alumni-student connections through mentorship, guidance, and professional development. While ASECCA links alumni to the institution, the Alumni Cell actively engages them with students, bridging the gap between academic growth and career achievement. With over 4000+ Alumni connections, 900+ group mentees, and significant digital engagement, our events attract active alumni participation, benefiting hundreds of students. Through panels, workshops, mentorship programs, and networking opportunities, we ensure a vibrant and accessible alumni network. Committed to empowering students, we strive to expand these connections, fostering a thriving, supportive community.
